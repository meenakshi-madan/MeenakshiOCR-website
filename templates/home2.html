High accuracy Android OCR app with features such as save, copy to clipboard, share, google, sms, recent history, etc.
Ever feel like you found an interesting piece of text in a book, a poster, a notice board... but you don't really want to type it all out? This is the app you need! All you need to do is click a picture and the app will get the text FOR you! You can then save it or share it, for example, and get back to it whenever you need it.
The advantage over using other OCR apps is that it's a simple lightweight app without unnecessary features, Internet connection is optional, and the accuracy is pretty high too.

Minimum android version required: 2.2 Froyo
