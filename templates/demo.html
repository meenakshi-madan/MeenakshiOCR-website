Image-to-text



Do you find yourself clicking pictures of notices, memos, posters, emails, website links, and other information often to come back to later? As easy as it is to capture text this way, sooner or later these images clutter up your gallery and occupy precious memory space. Like with most things, there's an app for that! With "Too Lazy To Type" you can quickly extract text from images in just a few clicks. No memory wastage. No typing. No excessive images to wade through.

Sci-fi Interface



Take a break from the minimalistic interfaces, walk into a bright, lively, and realistic display. Every single icon has been designed with great attention to detail and complementary colors. A pleasant backdrop to an extremely useful feature on a light app? What more could one ask for!

Recent History



Extracted an important piece of text recently but forgot to save it? Remembered you need to share text you extracted earlier with yet another application but you can't find the picture? No matter! The app stores the 5 most recently extracted text. You can share/google/save/copy/sms this text, or if you want to keep it a secret, you can also delete it.
